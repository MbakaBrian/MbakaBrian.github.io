About Us

DEYLIN ULTIMATE SPRINGS is a beverage manufacturing and distribution company that was established in April 2011 . The main manufacturing plant is situated in Naivasha along North Kinangop road with several distribution centres countrywide. Our headquaters are located in Nairobi Hurlingham Rose Avenue Street opposite Monarch hotel. Ours is a simple story; whatever we do, we do it well. Throughout the years we have strived to produce quality beverages for our esteemed customers using ultra-modern equipments, standard protocols and procedures with a sole purpose of attaining highest possible quality, and great satisfaction.

Our Mission

“To avail to all our customers quality & value for money in beverages using environmental friendly manufacturing processes and distribution channels that are both effecient and commercially viable.”

Our Vision

“To be the most re-knowned effecient manufacturer and distributor of quality beverages in East & Central Africa as well as The Great-Lake Region.”
