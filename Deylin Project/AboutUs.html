About Deylin Springs

Deylin Ultimate Springs Limited is a locally registered company dealing with the manufacture and distribution of non-alcoholic beverages. The Company has a manufacturing plant located on the Aberdare Range, North of Naivasha-Kenya. The manufacturing plant is equipped with various state of the art machinery designed to facilitate processes ranging from the extraction and purification of water, to the blending, packaging and storage of quality soft drinks ready for distribution and consumption.

Our Mission

To avail to all our customers quality and value for money beverages using environmentally friendly manufacturing processes and product distribution channels that are both efficient and commercially viable.

Our Vision

To be the most renowned and efficient manufacturer and distributor of quality beverages in the east African and Great Lakes region.

Our Standards

Our company’s processes are licensed by KEBS, and guided by WHO guidelines. We adhere to all quality requirements to ensure we give the best quality products.

Our Background

Deylin Ultimate Springs Limited was registered in 2012 and soon after licensed to extract, package and distribute non-alcoholic beverages. Initially the company focused on distribution of its packaged water to the Hotels and eateries in and around Naivasha town. Since then, the company’s product ranges have increased and distribution network grown to cover more counties, namely; Nairobi, Nakuru, Machakos, Kisii, Narok, Kajiado, Mombasa, Kwale and Voi.

Corporate Social Responsibility

We endeavor to be a good corporate citizen and continually look to give back to the community around us. The company currently provides treated water at affordable prices to communities around us, whose water is affected by high levels of fluoride.
